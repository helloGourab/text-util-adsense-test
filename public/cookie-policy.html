Cookie Policy

This policy explains how TxtUtils uses cookies and similar technologies.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site recognize you and remember certain information.

How We Use Cookies

TxtUtils does not use first-party cookies to track you. However, we use third-party services like Google AdSense which serve cookies to:

  • Show you relevant advertisements based on your interests.
  • Prevent you from seeing the same ad too many times.
  • Analyze traffic to understand how the site is performing.

Managing Cookies

You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings. You can also opt-out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
